Project Gallery Strategy

Full Home Renovation Pages Need Process, Not Just Photos.

Large renovations require more trust because the homeowner is choosing a team to manage complexity, budget, schedule, selections, and communication across many rooms.

What Makes This Lead Different.

Full home renovation inquiries are higher value, but they also carry more uncertainty. Homeowners need to see planning ability, communication structure, craftsmanship consistency, and a clear path from concept to completion.

How the Page Should Educate.

The page should break down project phases, rooms included, design decisions, problem solving, and final outcomes. This helps prospects understand the contractor's role before they ask for pricing.

How It Supports Growth.

A strong renovation page can support Google Ads for home remodeling leads, organic search, email nurture, estimate follow-up, and reactivation campaigns for older high-intent inquiries.

Renovation Gallery Assets

  • Room-by-room breakdown
  • Timeline and phase copy
  • Before-and-after proof
  • High-value inquiry qualification
