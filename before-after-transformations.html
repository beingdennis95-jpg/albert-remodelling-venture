Project Gallery Strategy

Before-and-After Pages Should Make the Value Obvious.

Transformations are powerful because they show contrast. The page should help homeowners understand what changed, why it mattered, and what level of work was required.

What the Transformation Should Explain.

A strong before-and-after page shows the original problem, the project goal, the scope, the constraints, the finished result, and how the remodel improved the home.

How Albert Uses It in Marketing.

These assets can support project gallery pages, Meta Ads, Google landing pages, social content, email nurture, and estimate follow-up. The same project becomes proof across the full growth system.

How It Helps Homeowners Decide.

When homeowners see clear contrast and understand the story behind the work, they feel safer reaching out. It turns craftsmanship into evidence, not just decoration.

Transformation Assets

  • Problem-to-solution story
  • Before-and-after sequence
  • Scope and finish notes
  • Social and follow-up content
